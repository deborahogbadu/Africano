What is Africano

Language is not just
communication.
It is memory infrastructure.

Nigeria alone has over 500 native tongues. Many are fading. Not because they were destroyed, but because they became inconvenient. Because schools taught us to be ashamed. Because technology was never built for our vowels.

Africano is being built to change that. One language at a time. One learner at a time.

Why It Matters
"When a language disappears, it is not only words that vanish.
It is worldview. Humour. Prayer. Philosophy.
A way of seeing the world that cannot be borrowed from anywhere else."

Language shame is real. It was placed there carefully, by systems that needed people to believe their own culture was inferior. Africano exists to undo that, quietly and deliberately.
